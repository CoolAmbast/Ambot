Frequently Asked Questions

What commands does Ambot support?
Ambot supports a wide range of commands including moderation, music playback, fun games, and utility functions. Use /help in your Discord server to see the full list of available commands.
Is Ambot really free?
Yes! Ambot is completely free to use with no paywalls. All features are available to everyone without any premium tiers or subscriptions.
How do I invite Ambot to my server?
You can invite Ambot by clicking the "Invite" button on our homepage. Make sure you have the necessary permissions in your Discord server to add bots.
What permissions does Ambot need?
Ambot requires basic permissions to function properly, including message management, voice channel access, and moderation capabilities. All permissions are clearly listed during the invitation process.
How can I get support?
Join our support server for direct assistance, bug reports, and feature requests. You can find the link in the navigation bar of our homepage or use the /support command in your server.
